# 📧 Phishing Email Analysis

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates how to identify and analyze a phishing email by examining common indicators of malicious intent and recommending appropriate security actions.

---

## Scenario

An employee receives an email claiming to be from the company's IT department requesting an urgent password reset through a provided link.

---

## Indicators of Phishing

- Unknown or suspicious sender
- Urgent or threatening language
- Suspicious links
- Requests for login credentials
- Grammar and spelling errors

---

## Risk Assessment

| Threat | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Level |
|---------|------------|--------|------------|
| Credential Theft | High | High | 🔴 Critical |
| Malware Infection | Medium | High | 🟠 High |

---

## Recommendations

- Do not click suspicious links.
- Verify the sender before responding.
- Report the email to the security team.
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
- Conduct user awareness training.
