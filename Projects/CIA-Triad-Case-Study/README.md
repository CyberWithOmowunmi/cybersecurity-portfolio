# 🔐 CIA Triad Case Study

## Project Overview

This case study explains how the CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability) supports information security through a practical business scenario.

---

## Scenario

A healthcare organization stores sensitive patient information electronically. Protecting this information is critical to maintaining trust and complying with regulations.

---

## Confidentiality

Patient records are protected using role-based access control (RBAC), encryption, and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to ensure that only authorized users can access sensitive information.

---

## Integrity

Data integrity is maintained through audit logs, file integrity monitoring, and regular backups to prevent unauthorized changes.

---

## Availability

Systems are kept available through regular backups, disaster recovery planning, redundant infrastructure, and timely software updates.

---

## Key Takeaways

The CIA Triad provides a foundation for protecting information systems by ensuring data remains confidential, accurate, and accessible when needed.
