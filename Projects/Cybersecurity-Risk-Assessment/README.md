# 🛡️ Cybersecurity Risk Assessment

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates a basic cybersecurity risk assessment based on a fictional company scenario. The objective is to identify valuable assets, evaluate potential threats and vulnerabilities, assess the level of risk, and recommend appropriate security controls to reduce those risks.

---

## Scenario

ABC Company is a small business that stores customer information, employee records, and financial data on its internal network. Employees access company resources using laptops connected to the corporate network.

---

## Assets

- Customer data
- Employee records
- Financial information
- Company laptops
- Internal network

---

## Threats

- Phishing attacks
- Malware
- Ransomware
- Unauthorized access
- Insider threats

---

## Vulnerabilities

- Weak passwords
- Lack of Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Employees not trained to identify phishing emails
- Outdated software
- Missing security updates

---

## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact |
|------|------------|--------|
| Phishing Attack | High | High |
| Malware Infection | Medium | High |
| Unauthorized Access | High | High |
| Data Loss | Medium | High |

---

## Recommendations

- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Enforce strong password policies
- Keep systems updated with security patches
- Conduct regular cybersecurity awareness training
- Install and maintain endpoint protection software
- Perform regular data backups
